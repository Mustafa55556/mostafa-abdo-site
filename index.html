My Projects

Smart Prosthetic Hand Project: I recently participated in implementing a prosthetic hand (artificial limb) project aimed at supporting people of determination who have lost their limbs. The project contributes to improving their quality of life through an artificial hand that helps them perform daily tasks. I had a role in preparing the model, writing part of the code, and assembling the technical components.
